I'm most grateful for easy access to a vast number of resources and local code camp communitites making my transition a reality. My favourite learning platforms are Free Code Camp & Lynda.com which complement each other perfectly! Spending 8 years in chemistry/industry I have a mind trained for problem solving and I get a buzz out of troubleshooting & finding solutions to problems. Occasionally I need help and thats ok, we all do at times and my collaborative nature allows me to be brave enough to ask when the time calls. I not only execute this skill in a corporate environment but naturally execute it in everyday life. In everything I participate in I ask myself "How can I make this better/easier/more efficient?" which lead me to discovering an aspect of my purpose in life, which is to be a problem solver.

I currently welcome all formats & resources that can support my growth in fulfilling Front End Developer requirements and I am actively seeking boot camp or training program opportunties. A mentor would be greatly appreciated!
